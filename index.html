About Our Gymnasium

Sports and Games are essential components, which help in building up a healthy intellectual life. The University has a well-equipped "Gymnasium" in the Campus with thirty-five exercise machines and a mini Multi-Gym for the students who aspire for physical well-being.

Separate time slots have been assigned to male students, female students, teachers and staff for maximizing the utility of the gymnasia. Stimulating activity teaches valuable physical skills, sets a pattern of lifelong fitness.

The gymnasia provide students with physical exercise consistently. It is particularly important for the well-being of students who have set aside physical activities after their school days.

Benefits of Gymnasium

A gymnasia workout features activities that raise the heart rate, increase the pulse rate and rev up metabolism. The physical exertion helps condition the mind — students can think more clearly and focus better following a good workout.

The physical exertion results in endorphin production in the brain. Endorphins are the "feel good" hormones that improve mood and outlook.

The physical activity becomes fun. The endorphins' effects allow students to proceed to their class feeling cheerful, relaxed and ready to focus on learning new ideas.
