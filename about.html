Rules and Regulations

Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University(CSJMU), Kanpur

Gym Rules & Regulations

  1. Persons interested in Gym membership will submit registration form along with two photographs (Passport Size).
  2. Entry should be marked in In/Out register every time before using the Gym facility.
  3. The Gym management reserves the right of Admission.
  4. Rs. 50/- will be charged for issuing duplicate I-Card.
  5. No Fee Refund in any Circumstances.
  6. Classes missed will not be adjusted.
  7. Membership is personal and non-transferable.
  8. The management shall reserve the right to choose part or all the facilities of the Centre due to repair, maintenance, expansions of facilities or special events, without advance notice and to readjust the operating hours of Gym Centre accordingly.
  9. Medical check-up is advised before joining.
  10. Any person entering the Gym using its facilities does so at its own risk. The Gym Centre cannot accept responsibility or liability for any injury or lose, including fatality or damage, whatsoever to any person or his/her property. Any person who suffers and accident or injury in the Gym Centre premises must report the accident or injury and the circumstances under which it occurred, to the in-charge immediately.
  11. No responsibility for personal belongings.
  12. Members should not undertake any physical activity for which they may not be fit and are responsible for monitoring their condition during exercise. The Gym Centre will not be in any harm to a member as a result of his undertaking physical activity within the centre.
  13. Each member is responsible for monitoring his or her own physical condition and physical activity undertaken at the gym Centre. Should any unusual symptoms occur, the member must immediately stop the activity and inform the instructor or any other member of the staff the Gym Centre.
  14. No workout after heavy meals.
  15. Avoid gym if suffering from illness or infection.
  16. Take instructions before using equipment.
  17. Consult doctor if having health issues.
  18. Members must enter into the Gym Centre only by the route indicated. Those found loitering in other areas, or creating thoroughfares will have their right of entry cancelled on the spot, without any refund of their fees
  19. Follow all notices displayed.
  20. Wear proper attire.
  21. No outside food allowed.
  22. No personal sound systems.
  23. Use gym in allotted time only.
  24. Equipment may stop during power failure.
  25. Use personal towels.
  26. Follow Sports Council rules.
  27. Carry water bottle, towel and kit.
  28. Irregular attendance may cancel membership.
  29. Declare medical condition.
  30. Use equipment under supervision.
  31. No responsibility for belongings.
  32. Discipline violation leads to cancellation.
  33. Keep gym clean.
  34. No spike shoes.
  35. First come first serve admission.
  36. Entry with clean shoes only.

Please note:

The kit should include: [Towel, Track Suit, Shoes, Water Bottle]
