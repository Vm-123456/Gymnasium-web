Revised Membership Fee for Gymnasium Centre

(w.e.f. 01/08/2023)

Sl. No. Category Monthly Fee Quarterly
1 Student / Research Scholar (Uni. Campus) Rs. 200/- Rs. 500/-
2 Teaching / Faculty Member / Administrative Officers & Family Rs. 400/- Rs. 1100/-
3 Non-Teaching Staff & Family Members Rs. 350/- Rs. 1000/-
4 Visitors / Outsiders Rs. 600/- Rs. 1500/-
5 Daily Guest Charges (Insiders) Rs. 20/- Per Hour
6 Daily Guest Charges (Outsiders) Rs. 50/- Per Hour

Note:

  • Prescribed fee is for each individual in each category.
  • Rs. 100/- will be charged extra for registration and I-Card.
  • Membership fee is non-refundable.

Note: The prescribed fee under each category for each individual shall be payable in advance along with application form.
