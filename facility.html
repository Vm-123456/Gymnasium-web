Facilities

Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University (CSJMU), Kanpur

Facilities to be provided under Sports Clinic

Functional Training for elderly any special population

Functional training is designed to improve strength, balance, flexibility, mobility, and overall physical function for elderly individuals and people with special health needs.

Mobility and Stability Training

Mobility and Stability Training focuses on improving joint movement, flexibility, balance, and body control to enhance overall physical performance and reduce the risk of injuries.

Gait Training Laboratory

The Gait Training Laboratory is a specialized facility dedicated to the assessment, analysis, and improvement of walking patterns and movement efficiency.

Treatment for Postural Imbalances

Postural imbalances can develop due to prolonged sitting, improper body mechanics, muscle weakness, repetitive movements, or lifestyle habits.

Body Mechanics Training

Body Mechanics Training focuses on teaching proper movement techniques and body positioning to perform daily activities, exercise routines, and occupational tasks safely and efficiently.

Strengthening and Conditioning Training to elite players of different sports

Our Strength and Conditioning Training program is specifically designed for elite athletes across various sports disciplines to enhance performance, maximize physical potential, and reduce the risk of injuries.

Development of Muscular Endurance and Strength

The Development of Muscular Endurance and Strength program is designed to improve the body's ability to perform physical activities efficiently while enhancing overall muscular performance.

Rehabilitation

Provide basic treatment and rehabilitation of sporting injuries and support performance through recovery and injury prevention.

Flexibility Training

Flexibility Training is designed to improve the range of motion of muscles and joints, enhancing overall mobility, posture, and physical performance.
