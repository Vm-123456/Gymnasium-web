Do and Don'ts

C.S.J.M. UNIVERSITY GYM CENTER
RULES AND REGULATIONS (do and don'ts)

***PLEASE BE COURTEOUS TO ALL USERS WHILE WORKING OUT.

ATTIRE AND FOOTWEAR
  • All gym users must wear properly fitted workout attire such as tank tops, T-shirts, shorts, leotards with running or cross training shoes.
  • Swimsuits, jeans and street clothes & shoes are NOT allowed.
  • Any attire causing safety issues or equipment damage is prohibited.
  • Dry, closed-toe athletic shoes or sneakers must be worn.
  • Outside footwear is prohibited on machines.
  • Use personal towels at all times.
  • Wear clean and dry clothes.
GYM EQUIPMENT
  • Maintain hygiene and proper gym etiquette.
  • Follow instructions while using equipment.
  • Consult staff if unsure about equipment use.
  • Keep conversations short while others are training.
CARDIO SECTION
  • Do not monopolize machines (max 20 minutes during peak hours).
  • Return safety key after use.
  • Do not use weights on cardio machines.
WEIGHTS TRAINING SECTION
  • Do not slam, drop or throw weights.
  • Return equipment to its original place.
  • Always use collars with barbells.
  • Maintain safe distance from others.
  • Work in pairs when required for safety.
  • Use equipment carefully to avoid injury or damage.
PROHIBITION
  • No food or drinks except water.
  • No smoking or alcohol.
PHYSICAL CONDITION
  • Consult doctor before starting workouts.
  • Always warm-up and cool-down properly.
  • Maintain correct posture and breathing.
  • Start with light weights to avoid injury.
PERSONAL BELONGINGS
  • Keep belongings in designated shelves.
  • No bags allowed on workout floor.
  • Management not responsible for valuables.
GENERAL
  • Do not paste notices or advertisements.
  • Do not touch equipment without permission.
  • Use gym at your own risk.
  • Follow all instructions given by staff.

USERS MUST EXIT FITNESS CENTER PROMPTLY BEFORE CLOSING TIME

*** University Gym reserves the right to modify rules at any time without prior notice ***
